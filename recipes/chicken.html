Chicken

Description

This is a recipes for chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, thyme)

Instructions

  1. Heat cast iron pan to medium high. Add oil.
  2. Add chicken to pan, skin side down until brown
  3. Cook the other side until brown
  4. Put entire pan into over and cook for 15 minutes at 350 degrees
